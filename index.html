My hometown of New Orleans is small by comparison of other international cities, yet feels so big with the constant influx of worldwide tourists. Small cities have their drawbacks and living in an area where you're two degrees of separation from everyone you know has its plusses and minuses. This might be why I love big city life. Anonymity.

When you find yourself being curious and on winding paths, it's easy for people to question why you're unable remain in a straight line. I find big cities are spots for multi-passionate individuals to not only thrive but, remain consistantly inspired.

For me, having lived in NYC and now L.A., the differences are many but the pulse of possibility remains the same. And that, is what keeps me going everyday.
